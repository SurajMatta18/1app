Our Business Model

What Do We Do?

1Apportunity is a two-sided marketplace — brands buy organic social media growth, and real users earn money by completing those tasks.

For Brands & Businesses

Brands purchase organic campaign packages — YouTube subscribers, Instagram followers, Google reviews, Amazon ratings & more. Every engagement comes from a real, verified Indian user — zero bots, zero fakes.

For Users & Earners

Users download our app and complete simple social media tasks — subscribe to a channel, follow a page, write a review, like a post. Each completed task earns real money, withdrawable instantly to UPI, Paytm, or bank.

We Connect Both Sides

1Apportunity is the bridge — we take campaign orders from businesses, break them into micro-tasks, and distribute them to our user base. Brands get genuine organic growth. Users get paid. Everyone wins.
